**NEW BASELINE**: Cost of Living index now uses **New York City = 100** (international standard), updated from the previous US national average.

#### 📊 Updated 2026 Cost of Living Data

All cities have been updated with the latest January 2026 data from Numbeo:

- Rent prices
- Home prices
- Cost of living indices
- Quality of life metrics

#### 🗺️ Regional Filtering

Find your ideal relocation destination by region:

- **7 Regions Available**: North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, Latin America, Middle East, Africa, Oceania
- **Multi-Region Selection**: Select multiple regions to narrow your search
- **Smart Defaults**: All regions selected by default for maximum discovery

**How to Use**:
1. Go to the **Recommendations** screen
2. Scroll to "Filter by Region"
3. Tap region chips to select (blue = selected, grey = unselected)
4. Tap "Find My Ideal Cities" to see filtered results
5. Use "Show All" to reset to all regions

#### 💼 Employer Relocation Assistance Calculator

Planning a move with company support? Calculate your real out-of-pocket costs:

- **Toggle Feature**: Turn on/off employer assistance
- **Adjustable Coverage**: Slide from 0% to 100% employer contribution
- **Clear Breakdown**: See total cost, employer contribution, and your final cost

**Example**:
- Total Moving Cost: $8,500
- Employer Covers: 75% (-$6,375)
- **Your Cost: $2,125**

**How to Use**:
1. Go to **Moving Cost Estimator**
2. Enter your move details
3. Toggle "Employer relocation assistance?" ON
4. Adjust the percentage slider (default: 50%)
5. See your real cost instantly

## How to Use New Features

### Comparing International Cities

1. Go to **Salary Calculator**
2. Select your current city (e.g., San Francisco, CA)
3. Select your target city (e.g., Bangkok, Thailand)
4. Enter your current salary
5. Tap "Calculate"

You'll see:
- Cost of living comparison
- Tax breakdown for both countries
- Equivalent salary needed in target city
- Purchasing power gain or loss
- Currency conversions

### Finding Cities by Region

1. Go to **Recommendations**
2. Set your preferences (lifestyle priorities)
3. Scroll to "Filter by Region"
4. Select desired regions (e.g., Europe + Oceania)
5. Tap "Find My Ideal Cities"

Results will only show cities from selected regions.

### Calculating Move with Employer Help

1. Go to **Moving Cost Estimator**
2. Select cities and home size
3. Toggle "Employer relocation assistance?" ON
4. Adjust slider to match your offer (e.g., 75%)
5. See your out-of-pocket cost
